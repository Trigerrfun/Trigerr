How to Play

Objective: The goal is to checkmate your opponent's king. This means the king is in a position to be captured ('in check') and cannot escape capture.

Controls:

  • Click on one of your pieces to select it.
  • Valid moves will be highlighted on the board.
  • Click on a highlighted square to move your piece.

Modes: You can play against the computer or against another player on the same device.
